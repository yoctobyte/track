Position in TRACK

  • Agent: laptop or controlled device, with local privileges where needed.
  • Hub: central database and service surface.
  • Web: operator-facing summary and download surface.

This keeps privileged inspection out of the public-facing web app while still letting TRACK integrate network evidence with rooms, racks, ports, and device documentation later.

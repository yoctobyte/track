TRACK is an archival reverse-engineering system for real-world technical
environments, viewed through the eyes of a technical admin.

That means:

- the core job is documentation, not just control
- any technical object or situation is in scope:
    - rooms
    - devices
    - cables
    - racks
    - manuals
    - labels
    - procedures
    - network topology
    - media players
    - mysterious black boxes
- capture comes first:
    - pictures
    - notes
    - documents
    - voice
    - scans
    - identifiers
- structure comes next:
    - entities
    - relationships
    - ownership
    - location
    - history
- control is one layer among others:
    - Ansible
    - media player actions
    - network scanning
    - remote operations
- the long-term unifying view is spatial and temporal:
    - tie evidence together in approximate 3D
    - track changes over time
    - effectively reverse-engineer the museum as a living technical system
